COVID-19 swab tests
One year of results from a single italian pharmacy

An interactive data visualization of how the number of swabs per day changes in relation to DPCM.
A subplot investigates the changes in the average age of tested people in relation to the time of the year
and the daily average temperature.

64 060 swab test (on 21 461 people)
 Data are been integrated with historical weather data to compute average temperature (source: https://www.3bmeteo.com/)
 Data was collected from a pharmacy in the province of Padua
 Pharmacy offers swab test for Covid-19 since January 20, 2021
Data visualization updated at December 31, 2021

 Interactive data visualization allows zooming by chart region selection and filtering by clicking on legend labels
Note:
* Average age is computed as rolling mean in 30 days
* Error bars in average age indicate 95% confidence interval
